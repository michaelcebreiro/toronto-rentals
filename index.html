★ Why 110 & 120 Broadway stay top of the list: The building is the main draw. A great gym, indoor + outdoor pool, sauna, steam room, and basketball court function as an extension of the apartment - meaning the unit doesn’t have to do everything on its own. If the amenities are poor, you’re effectively locked inside your apartment. The alternative would be a GoodLife membership (discounted through Michael’s girlfriend, but still an added cost) and having to leave the building every time you want a proper workout. Broadway removes that problem entirely.
⚠ 110 & 120 Broadway - application risk: Building management has been cautious about approving applicants without Canadian credit history or a local guarantor. Michael is a UK national with no Canadian credit file. No rejection issued, but Kenan is not confident of a yes. Treat all Broadway offers as uncertain until confirmed. If Broadway falls through, 39 Roehampton (E2 Condos) is the most realistic landing spot.
