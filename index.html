★ Why 110 & 120 Broadway stay top of the list: The building is the main draw. A great gym, indoor + outdoor pool, sauna, and basketball court function as an extension of the apartment — meaning the unit doesn’t have to do everything on its own. If the amenities are poor, you’re effectively locked inside your apartment. The alternative would be a GoodLife membership (discounted through Kenan’s girlfriend, but still an added cost) and having to leave the building every time you want a proper workout. Broadway removes that problem entirely.

Michael is a UK national with no Canadian credit file. No rejection issued, but Kenan is not confident of a yes. Treat all Broadway offers as uncertain until confirmed.
